Who is Khánh Linh?

To some people, I’m known as a contestant from The Face, a model, or a fashion lover. The truth is, I’m simply an experience seeker, head blooming with questions and the courage to go where my curiosity would take me.


Hence I don’t waste my time with what-if’s and regrets, but remain on my path despite challenges because ‘wrong decisions’ are simply ‘learned lessons’ in disguise.


As I go about my journey, exploring as well as learning about fashion, beauty and travel, I would love to share my experience with you all because knowledge is the gift that keeps on giving.


Stay Gorgeous,
KL.
